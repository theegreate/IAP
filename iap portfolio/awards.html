    • Ariana Grande, an American singer and actress, has received several awards and distinctions for her music and contributions to the entertainment industry. Spanning her successful career, she has won numerous Grammy Awards, MTV Music Awards, and World Music Awards, among many others. Grande has also been recognized for her philanthropic work and activism, earning the Presidential Medal of Freedom, the highest civilian honor in the United States.
