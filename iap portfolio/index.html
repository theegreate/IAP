Biography

Education: Ariana Grande is an American pop singer and actress. She was born on June 26, 1993, in Burbank, California. Grande graduated from Pacific Christian High School in 2011 and later attended Los Angeles State University for two years before dropping out to focus on her career. She has since gone on to become one of the most successful artists in the music industry, known for her powerful vocals and hit songs. Despite not completing a formal education, Grande has continued to learn and grow throughout her career and has been involved in various philanthropic efforts.

Employment History: Ariana Grande is a renowned American singer, songwriter, actress, and producer. She started her career in theatre before gaining fame as a member of the Nickelodeon TV show "Victorious" in 2010. Since then, she has released six studio albums, including "Thank U, Next," which received critical and commercial success. In addition to her music career, she has also appeared in various TV shows and movies, such as "MacGyver," "Transformers: Dark of the Moon," and "Last Look." As of now, she continues to tour and record new music.
